<analysis>
HexaBid is an AI-powered Tender ERP SaaS that automates the discovery, analysis, and management of public tenders for Indian contractors, OEMs, and service providers.
It integrates AI/ML, document automation, and competitive intelligence into a single cloud platform optimized for lightweight deployment (AWS Free Tier or small VPS).

<product_requirements>
Core Objectives

Enable users to search, analyze, and bid on tenders efficiently.

Provide AI insights: tender summaries, risk analysis, win prediction, pricing recommendations.

Offer an OEM collaboration hub for product mapping and vendor communication.

Deliver a subscription-based SaaS model with billing, user management, and analytics.

Maintain low-resource operability for small-scale users and startups.

Functional Goals

Tender search and filtering (keyword, region, department, value)

AI-assisted tender analysis and document parsing (OCR + NLP)

Competitor tracking and win probability prediction

Product and OEM suggestion engine

Price estimation and cost breakdown (BoQ analysis)

Workspace collaboration, document automation, and alerts

MIS dashboards and tender analytics

Role-based access, subscription billing, and admin panel
</product_requirements>
